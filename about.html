About B.N.M. & CO.

Learn about our history, mission, and the values that drive our firm.

Our Story

Founded over 20 years ago in Deoghar, Jharkhand , BNM & Co. was established with a commitment to providing exceptional chartered accounting services based on integrity, expertise, and client-centricity. Over the years, we have grown and adapted to the evolving financial landscape, building strong relationships with our clients and helping them navigate complex financial challenges.

We pride ourselves on our deep understanding of Indian financial regulations and our ability to provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of each client, from individuals to large corporations.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to empower our clients with the financial knowledge and support they need to achieve their goals. We strive to be more than just accountants; we aim to be trusted partners who contribute to your long-term success.

Our core values include:

  • **Integrity:** Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our dealings.
  • **Expertise:** Continuously enhancing our knowledge and skills to provide the best advice.
  • **Client Focus:** Putting our clients' needs and success at the forefront of everything we do.
  • **Innovation:** Embracing new technologies and approaches to deliver efficient services.
